Each tile is an independent take exported from the scene-animator. Selecting one snaps the canvas to the moment right before that take starts, then plays it. Dialogue bubbles you place below belong only to the currently selected scene. In the exported production, a line only appears after that scene's animation finishes and you click; it fades in, holds for however long you set, then fades itself out — and you click again to bring up the next line.
